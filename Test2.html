MODERN means : "May Our Determined Efforts Reward New generation".

MODERN ENGLISH SCHOOL , A Co-educational residential school situated at J.M. Goenka Road (Rajbari) in the town of KURSEONG in the Hill District of Darjeeling. The School was established in the year 1992 by a prominent Social leader of this Hill District Sri P.C. Agarwal. It is located on motorable road , 10 minutes walk from Taxi stand / Railway Station and in a very peaceful & safe area.
VISION WITH A MISSION: The Vision is to provide high quality all round education and to have personalized look after of the child. And the MISSION statement is "A PROMISING FUTURE" by offering integrated and holistic education to equip the students with the relevant skills ,knowledge and self assurance which will provide them with an effective platform for the enhancement for the future success in their careers and personal life. The opportunity for development through International and cross cultural in fresh and healthy environment.
SCHOOL CAMPUS : The School campus is within an area of 20000 Sq.ft. with fully secured boundary wall all round the campus. Three buildings of 4/5 storied ,covering about 18 thousand floor area , has spacious rooms for Classes , Library , Laboratory , Computer (with Internet facility) , Billiard , Office with Parlor. A play field , Indoor big Hall for games & other activities.
TEACHERS : All the teachers are fully qualified with plenty of experience in teaching. They are well behaved , dedicated and hard working . Each class is having not more than 25 students , enabling the teachers to devote complete attention to each student. Mr.ASHWANI AGARWAL , a very experienced with a pleasing nature is the Principal of the school .
I.C.S.E. RESULTS : Since Affiliated with ICSE Board , the results of last 3 batch (since 2006) is far from excellence .All the students were passed where the average is 76% and many students got above 90%.The 2009 batch is expected to do much better results. Now the school is having its HOME CENTRE and will give ICSE examination at school only.
